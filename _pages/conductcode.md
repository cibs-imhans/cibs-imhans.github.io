# Code of Conduct for the participants of Sequence to Synapse
**Organized by IMHANS, Kerala**
---

## Purpose
This workshop is held at a **mental health institute**, and it is essential to maintain **respect, sensitivity, and discipline** at all times. This Code of Conduct ensures a **safe, supportive, and positive learning environment** for participants, staff, and patients.

---

## Expected Behavior
All participants are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**  
  Treat fellow students, faculty, staff, and patients with kindness and respect, regardless of background or experience. Use polite and inclusive language.

- **Maintain Discipline and Decorum**  
  Speak softly and behave professionally. Avoid loud conversations, jokes, or actions that may disturb others within the institute.

- **Be Attentive and Engaged**  
  Pay attention during sessions. Keep mobile phones on silent mode. Avoid side conversations, phone usage, or unrelated activities during talks and hands-on sessions.

- **Be Punctual and Prepared**  
  Arrive on time for all sessions and follow the workshop schedule. Bring required materials and be ready to participate.

---

## Prohibited Behavior
The following are **strictly prohibited**:

- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind.
- Sharing personal, clinical, or confidential information related to the institute, patients, or participants.
- **Photography, videography, or recording in areas where patients are present or receive care.**
- Taking photos or videos anywhere on campus **without prior permission from the organizers**.
- Use of alcohol, drugs, smoking, or vaping anywhere on the institute premises.
- Any behavior that may disturb patients or compromise the instituteâ€™s therapeutic environment.

---

## Reporting and Consequences

- Report any inappropriate behavior or violations to the organizers immediately.
- Organizers will take appropriate action, which may include a warning, removal from the workshop, or restriction from future events.
- Serious violations may be escalated to institute authorities or security.

---

## Agreement
By participating in this workshop, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.  
Thank you for helping maintain a **respectful, safe, and learning-focused environment**.
